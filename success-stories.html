Case Studies

Success Stories

Real-world results across industries. Each project delivered measurable business outcomes at scale.

01. AI-Powered HR Evaluation Engine

Challenge

Manual candidate evaluation was bottlenecked. Creating custom role assessment sheets required 4 hours, and manual fit reporting consumed 2 hours per applicant.

Solution

Designed an AI Question Engine that programmatically structures custom evaluation forms based on seniority, role, and sector parameters, scoring applicants instantly via backend integrations.

Business Impact

Slashed role setup and assessment time from 4 hours to under 2 minutes. Enabled client to scale candidate evaluation without increasing HR headcount.

02. AI FDA Documentation for Pharma

Challenge

Manually compiling 10,000+ pages of scientific research into 200-page FDA dossiers required 2 domain experts roughly 4 months and was highly prone to citation errors.

Solution

Developed an AI document synthesis pipeline to extract, index, and organize research databases, generating compliant dossier sections alongside a custom review interface for human verification.

Business Impact

Reduced compilation time from 16 weeks to just 2 weeks. Enabled 95% automated generation with only 5% requiring direct manual verification.

03. Lead Prioritization & Voice AI Caller

Challenge

An automobile finance company was overwhelmed with incoming leads, many of which were low quality. Sales representatives struggled to identify high-probability prospects.

Solution

Built a credit propensity and lead scoring model using customer profiles, location, and vehicle types. Integrated scoring into CRM and deployed a voice-based LLM telephony agent to qualify leads automatically.

Business Impact

Generated an 18% revenue lift across dealerships in 3 months by ensuring agents only call pre-qualified leads, reducing cold calling hours by 40%.

04. Reporting Platform for Interview Provider

Challenge

An online interview provider struggled with slow, manual candidate performance reports, leading to delays in client hiring cycles and high operational overhead.

Solution

Developed an automated end-to-end data pipeline and reporting system that aggregates candidate metrics, interview evaluations, and feedback into client-facing interactive dashboards.

Business Impact

Eliminated reporting delays entirely by automating metric compilation. Clients gain self-service access to candidate profiles instantly, accelerating time-to-hire by 90%.

05. ROAS Optimization for E-Commerce

Challenge

Siloed marketing databases made it difficult to attribute revenue to ad spend, leading to inefficient budget allocation and wasted customer acquisition costs.

Solution

Created a unified attribution model linking product inventory and marketing acquisition data. Built interactive analytical dashboard panels for real-time ROAS and LTV monitoring.

Business Impact

Slashed wasted marketing expenditure by 22% in the first 30 days while boosting blended return on ad spend (ROAS) to 1.3x over 6 weeks.

06. Propensity Loan Cross-Selling

Challenge

Untargeted marketing campaigns led to low cross-sell conversions and high acquisition costs for two-wheeler and personal loan products.

Solution

Built a unified customer profile model to evaluate cross-sell propensity scores. Integrated automated marketing tools (WhatsApp, Email, CleverTap) to deliver targeted user campaigns.

Business Impact

Boosted successful loan cross-sell conversions by 18% inside 2 months, lowering cost-per-acquisition (CPA) by 15% via channels optimization.

07. ML-Driven CRM Reactivation Pipeline

Challenge

A large dormant user base and inactive rechargers led to missed revenue, with generic notification campaigns failing to drive engagement.

Solution

Built a churn prediction model to score recharge probability. Deployed A/B-tested reactivation campaigns with personalized incentives.

Business Impact

Delivered up to an 11x revenue uplift in targeted dormant cohorts and automated scoring updates directly in the production database.

08. CLTV Prediction & High-Value User Identification

Challenge

Lack of early predictive visibility on newly acquired user value made it difficult to optimize KPIs and allocate marketing spend.

Solution

Built CLTV models combining early behavioral app event data to predict high-value status within 30 days of signup.

Business Impact

Established predictive LTV as an active operational KPI, providing marketing teams with automated database tags for immediate personalization.

09. E-Commerce Operations & Reporting Automation

Challenge

Manual daily extraction of Shopify sales and ad spend data (Meta, Google) created reporting bottlenecks and delayed executive insights.

Solution

Integrated Shopify and marketing APIs to auto-populate a master dashboard, combined with an automated bot for automated daily performance updates.

Business Impact

Saved hundreds of operational hours yearly and enabled instant pacing visibility toward a $2.5M revenue target.
